Reach Out for Help!

Our hotline advocates are here to provide personalized, non-judgmental support tailored to your needs. Whether you want to discuss your safety, learn about available resources in your community, or connect with our programs and services, we're here for you.

  • Crisis counseling
  • Safety planning
  • Help finding crisis shelters
  • Information on local resources

We’re here to assist you in any language. Don’t hesitate to call us now.

OUR HOTLINES

DOMESTIC VIOLENCE

+234 909 555 1111

For help with domestic violence, please call this hotline.

CRIME VICTIMS

+234 803 876 2671

For help with all crimes, including support for family members of homicide victims, please call this hotline.

RAPE AND SEXUAL ASSAULTS

+234 818 724 3468

For help with rape and sexual assault, please call this hotline, or call NYC’s 311. In an emergency, call 911.
